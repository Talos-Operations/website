About us

Big-business marketing, small-business budget

Talos Operations gives local businesses the websites, Google presence, and automation that used to be reserved for companies with deep pockets and full-time marketing teams.

Our mission

Turn local businesses into the obvious choice

A great plumber or HVAC company shouldn't lose jobs to a competitor with a flashier website and better Google ranking. We close that gap. We build what agencies charge five figures for — faster, sharper, and at a price that makes sense for a local business.

And we don't stop at marketing. We're building software — like Deed Reader for law firms — that takes the same idea into whole new industries.

What we do, in a sentence

We build websites and Google presence for local trades, automate their follow-up, and build software products for new industries.
