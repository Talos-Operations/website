The problem

Deeds are slow, manual, and easy to get wrong

Paralegals burn hours reading deeds and title documents by hand — pulling parcel IDs, parties, legal descriptions, and recording dates into intake forms. It's tedious, expensive, and one typo can derail a closing.

Deed Reader does it in seconds. Upload a document and it extracts the details your team needs into clean, structured intake you can trust.

What it does

Built for the way firms actually work

Reads any deed
