What are citations?

A citation is any place online that lists your business’s name, address, and phone number — directories like Yelp, Apple Maps, Bing, BBB, Facebook, and dozens of local and industry sites.

Why they matter

Google decides who to trust partly by how consistently your business shows up across the web. The more accurate, matching listings it finds, the more established and legitimate you look — which lifts you in local search and the map pack.

Why they’re worth paying for

  • They stay with you forever. Once a citation is built, it’s yours — it keeps working even if you stop every other service.
  • One-time cost, long-term payoff. You pay once; the listing keeps feeding your rankings for years.
  • They compound. Every consistent listing is another vote of trust — the more you have, the stronger your local presence.
  • They’re foundational. Ads bring you leads today; citations quietly strengthen your visibility for the long haul.

Bottom line: citations are one of the best long-term investments in your local presence — cheap, permanent, and always working in the background.
